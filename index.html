Sports

When there no longer was snow, I ran 🏃🏽‍♀️, hiked in the mountains 🥾, did weight training 🏋🏽‍♀️, mountain biking 🚵🏽‍♀️, road biking 🚴🏽‍♀️, swimming 🏊🏽‍♀️ and wheel-skiing.
